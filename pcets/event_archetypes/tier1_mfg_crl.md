# Archetype: Tier 1A — Manufacturing-Only CRL

## Definition
FDA issues a Complete Response Letter citing manufacturing or facility deficiencies **only**. Drug efficacy, safety profile, and clinical data are NOT challenged.

## Identification Criteria
- CRL letter specifies CMC (chemistry, manufacturing, controls) issues only
- No clinical hold issued
- No new safety signal
- No efficacy concern stated
- Company confirms resubmission path with defined timeline
