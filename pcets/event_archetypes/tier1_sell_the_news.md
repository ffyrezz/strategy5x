# Archetype: Tier 1B — Sell-the-News Approval

## Definition
Drug receives FDA approval but stock sells off due to overhang clearing, profit-taking, short-seller activity, or market cap concerns. Approval is clean and unambiguous.

## Identification Criteria
- Clean FDA approval (no black box warning added, no REMS surprise)
- Stock sold off >15% post-approval within T+0 to T+2
- Prior run contamination absent (SC8 check: <10% in T-14 to T-0)
- Institutional ownership intact (no 13F selling)

| Ticker | Date | T+7 | Notes |

| TGTX (ublituximab) | 2022-12-28 | +37.7% | Highest single return in B1-B11 |
| APLS (pegcetacoplan) | 2023-02-17 | +15.3% | Bear regime — archetype works across regimes |

## PCETS Verdict
**High-conviction BUY.** SC floor ≥8 required. Check institutional ownership (SC6) carefully — selling-on-approval is a Dead Cat signal.

## Failure Conditions
- Institutional selling post-approval → Dead Cat signal, avoid
- Competing drug approval within 30 days → SC9 contamination
- Market cap >$10B at approval → overhang less likely to clear quickly
